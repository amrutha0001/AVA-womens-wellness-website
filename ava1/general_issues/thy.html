Understanding Thyroid Health

About Your Thyroid

The thyroid is a butterfly-shaped gindex.php in your neck that regulates metabolism, energy, and many bodily functions. When it's not working properly, it can affect your entire body.

Common Conditions

Hypothyroidism (underactive thyroid) and hyperthyroidism (overactive thyroid) are the most common disorders. Women are 5-8 times more likely to develop thyroid problems.

Nutrition Tips

Foods rich in selenium, zinc, iodine, and omega-3s support thyroid health. Limit processed foods and consider reducing gluten if you have Hashimoto's thyroiditis.

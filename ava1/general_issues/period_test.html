Understanding Your Cycle

What's Normal?

A typical menstrual cycle lasts 21-35 days, with bleeding for 2-7 days. Variations can occur due to stress, diet, exercise, or health conditions.

When to Seek Help

Consult a doctor if you experience: cycles shorter than 21 days or longer than 35 days, bleeding for more than 7 days, or severe pain that disrupts your life.

Nutrition Tips

Foods rich in iron, omega-3s, and magnesium can support menstrual health. Stay hydrated and limit caffeine and salty foods during your period.

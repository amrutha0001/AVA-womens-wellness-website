Turmeric Salmon with Greens

Rich in omega-3s to reduce inflammation and support hormone health

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Servings: 2

PCOS Benefits

High in anti-inflammatory omega-3 fatty acids, rich in antioxidants from turmeric, and packed with nutrient-dense greens to support hormone balance.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with a side of quinoa for complete protein
  • Add avocado slices for healthy fats
  • Drizzle with lemon-tahini dressing
  • Pair with fermented vegetables for gut health

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups mixed greens (kale, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Pat salmon dry and place on baking sheet.
  3. Mix turmeric, cumin, garlic powder, pepper and salt in small bowl.
  4. Rub salmon with olive oil, then sprinkle spice mixture evenly over fillets.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily.
  6. Toss greens with lemon juice, herbs, and optional sweetener.
  7. Serve salmon over bed of dressed greens.

Quinoa & Roasted Veggie Bowl

A nutritionist-designed meal to support hormone balance and metabolic health

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Servings: 4

PCOS Benefits

15g of fiber to support gut health, plant-based proteins for sustained energy, and low-glycemic ingredients to regulate blood sugar.

Serving Suggestions

  • Top with avocado for healthy fats
  • Add hemp seeds for extra protein
  • Drizzle with tahini-lemon dressing
  • Pair with fermented vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tri-color quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 medium sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Sea salt & black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line baking sheet.
  2. Cook quinoa in broth for 15 minutes. Let stand.
  3. Toss vegetables with oil and spices. Roast 25-30 mins.
  4. Fluff quinoa and divide among bowls.
  5. Top with roasted vegetables and garnish.

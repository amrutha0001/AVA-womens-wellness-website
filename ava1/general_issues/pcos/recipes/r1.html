Spiced Tofu & Vegetable Stir-Fry

Low-glycemic, high-protein meal with blood sugar balancing spices

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Servings: 3

PCOS Benefits

Contains phytoestrogen-rich tofu to help balance hormones, fiber-packed vegetables for gut health, and blood sugar-regulating spices like cinnamon and fenugreek.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with cauliflower rice for low-carb option
  • Top with crushed peanuts for extra crunch
  • Add a side of kimchi for probiotics
  • Drizzle with sesame oil for healthy fats

Ingredients

  • 14 oz extra firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas)
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp fenugreek powder
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp tamari or coconut aminos
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • ¼ cup fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Press tofu for 15 minutes, then cube into 1-inch pieces
  2. Heat 1 tbsp coconut oil in large pan over medium-high heat
  3. Add tofu and cook until golden on all sides (8-10 mins)
  4. Remove tofu and add remaining oil to pan
  5. Sauté ginger and garlic for 30 seconds until fragrant
  6. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until crisp-tender
  7. Mix in spices, tamari, and rice vinegar
  8. Return tofu to pan and toss to combine
  9. Finish with sesame oil, sesame seeds, and fresh basil
